## Key Findings (Weighted View)

### Copilot (Microsoft 365) — 56/100
- Integration strength keeps it competitive.  
- Loses ground due to poor fidelity with Markdown/JSON/YAML and limited KB depth.  

### SysAid Copilot — 55/100
- Strongest ease-of-use (setup + maintenance).  
- Limited ITSM scope reduces practical accuracy and breadth.  

### Rovo AI (Confluence) — 68/100
- Scores highest thanks to speed, accuracy, and wide connector coverage.  
- Best fit for wiki-driven and SaaS-heavy environments.  

---

## Conclusion
- **Rovo AI** leads overall (68/100).  
- **Copilot (MS)** is strong in breadth of integration.  
- **SysAid Copilot** remains best for ITSM-focused teams.
